Overview

Purpose

To offer fun and inexpensive rafting experiences to all who want to know the exhileration of the most adrenaline spiking sport on the planet! With enough following and income, this business will be able to grow large enough to but a raft in every river.

Audience

  • What age is ideal for rafting?
  • Any Age can participate! But the more advanced trails are meant for younger and stronger adults.
  • Does it cost a lot of money?
  • Rafting is affordable for anybody who is looking for something to do on the weekend to take a break from the monotonous 9-5, in highschool looking for a fun summer activity, or even the retired who have nothing to do in general.
  • Why this company?
  • Most companies focus all their attention on the younger adults who can handle longer and rougher terrains. We are here to make this invaluable experience available for everyone!
